How to Make Veggie Maggie

The Perfect Midnight Snack

Ingredients:

  • 1 pack Maggi noodles
  • 1 cup water
  • 1/4 cup chopped onions
  • 1/4 cup chopped tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup chopped capsicum
  • 1/4 tsp turmeric
  • 1/4 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp oil or butter
  • Maggi tastemaker

Steps:

  1. Heat oil in a pan.
  2. Add onions, saut√© for a minute.
  3. Add tomatoes and capsicum, cook till soft.
  4. Add turmeric and chili powder.
  5. Add 1 cup water and bring to boil.
  6. Add noodles and tastemaker.
  7. Cook for 2-3 minutes, stir occasionally.
  8. now your mid night cravings are no more
